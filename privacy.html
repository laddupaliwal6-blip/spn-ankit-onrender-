Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Our Privacy Commitment

QuizMania is a free quiz platform. We do not require account signup and we are committed to protecting your privacy by minimizing data collection.

Data We Collect

  • Local data: quiz activity and recent session info are stored in your browser's localStorage to track recent quizzes. This data stays on your device and is not personally identifiable.
  • Contact submissions: if you choose to contact us via the form, we receive the information you submit to respond to your inquiry.

Cookies and Ads

We do not use cookies for tracking and the site is ad-free to ensure a focused, secure experience.

Your Choices

  • You can clear your browser's site data at any time to remove any locally stored quiz activity.
  • Using your browser's private or incognito mode will prevent any session data from being stored.

Contact

Questions? Use the contact form on the Home page to reach us.
