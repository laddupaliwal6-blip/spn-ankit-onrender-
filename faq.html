FAQ

Answers to common questions about playing quizzes on QuizMania.

Is QuizMania a safe platform?

Yes. QuizMania offers an ad-free, family-friendly environment with no in-app purchases, focused on fair trivia play.

Can I save my scores?

Yes. Your recent quiz sessions are stored locally in your browser so you can track progress and review past rounds.

Can I play on mobile devices?

Absolutely. QuizMania is responsive and works smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktop computers.

How do quizzes work?

Pick a category, answer each question within the time limit, and submit your answers. Quizzes support touch and mouse input.

Is QuizMania free to use?

Yes. QuizMania is completely free—designed to offer inclusive access to entertaining quizzes for everyone.
